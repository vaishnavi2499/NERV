Anti-Doping Beginner

Level 1 serves as the foundation for understanding the basics of anti-doping. This level is designed for individuals who are new to the concept of anti-doping, such as young athletes, beginners in sports, or those unfamiliar with the regulations. The focus is on introducing key terms, the importance of clean sport, and the basic rules regarding banned substances and practices. It aims to build awareness and promote a culture of fair play.

Ethical Athlete

Level 2 delves deeper into the rules and regulations surrounding anti-doping practices. This level is aimed at athletes, coaches, and support staff who need a more detailed understanding of anti-doping regulations, including the procedures for drug testing and the consequences of violations. The content covers the roles and responsibilities of athletes, the therapeutic use exemptions (TUE), and the testing process.

Level 3 is designed for those who already have a solid understanding of anti-doping regulations and are looking to deepen their knowledge.
